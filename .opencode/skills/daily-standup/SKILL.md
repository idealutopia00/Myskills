## What I do
- Collect information about yesterday's completed work
- Gather today's planned tasks
- Identify blockers and issues
- Generate structured standup notes in a consistent format
- Suggest follow-up actions and dependencies

## When to use me
Use this skill during daily standup meetings or when preparing individual standup updates. I help maintain consistent reporting across teams and track progress over time.

## How to use me
1. Provide details about yesterday's completed tasks
2. Share today's planned work items
3. Mention any blockers or challenges
4. I'll organize this into clear standup notes
5. You can request specific formats (bullet points, tables, etc.)

## Output format
- **Yesterday**: Completed tasks with brief descriptions
- **Today**: Planned work items with priorities
- **Blockers**: Issues needing attention
- **Dependencies**: Team coordination needs
- **Notes**: Additional context or follow-ups
