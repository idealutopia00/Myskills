## What I do
- Extract key points from meeting transcripts or discussions
- Identify decisions made and action items assigned
- Track follow-up tasks with owners and deadlines
- Organize notes into logical sections
- Highlight important discussions and outcomes

## When to use me
Use this skill after meetings to create structured notes that capture what was discussed, decided, and needs to be done. I'm useful for team meetings, client calls, planning sessions, and any collaborative discussions.

## How to use me
1. Provide meeting transcript, recording, or summary
2. Specify participants and meeting context
3. I'll analyze the content and extract key information
4. You can request specific formats or additional details

## Output format
- **Meeting Details**: Date, time, participants, purpose
- **Agenda Items**: Topics discussed
- **Decisions Made**: Key conclusions and choices
- **Action Items**: Tasks with owners and deadlines
- **Discussion Summary**: Main points and arguments
- **Next Steps**: Follow-up actions and future meetings
- **Notes**: Additional context or references
