## What I do
- Analyze academic papers, articles, or research documents
- Extract key information: abstract, methodology, results, conclusions
- Identify important contributions and limitations
- Generate structured reading notes with sections: summary, key findings, methodology, critique, future work
- Provide citation information in standard formats (APA, MLA, etc.)
- Highlight connections to related work

## When to use me
Use this skill when you have academic papers or research documents that need thorough analysis and note-taking. I'm particularly useful for literature reviews, research paper summaries, and preparing for academic discussions.

## How to use me
1. Provide the document (PDF, text, or URL) or paste the content
2. Specify any focus areas or specific questions you want answered
3. I'll analyze the content and produce detailed reading notes
4. You can request adjustments or additional analysis

## Output format
- **Title and Authors**: Basic publication information
- **Abstract Summary**: Concise overview
- **Key Contributions**: Main findings and innovations
- **Methodology**: Research approach and techniques
- **Results**: Important data and outcomes
- **Critique**: Strengths, weaknesses, limitations
- **Connections**: Related works and context
- **Citations**: Proper reference format
- **Tags**: Keywords for organization
